Chronios Vale is committed to maintaining a safe, productive, and supportive working environment for all employees.

Human Resources Contact Information

If you are experiencing workplace difficulties, interpersonal conflict, or require assistance regarding internal procedures, please contact the Human Resources department using the appropriate channels below.

  • Email: hr@chroniosvale.org
  • Internal Extension: 441
  • Office Hours: 08:00 - 18:00
  • Emergency Staff Support Line: EXT-09

Common Requests

Employees may contact HR regarding payroll enquiries, onboarding support, workplace conduct concerns, wellbeing services, or access permission issues.

Before submitting a support request, employees are encouraged to complete all required onboarding documentation and policy acknowledgement modules.

New employees who have not completed onboarding procedures may experience restricted access to internal systems.

Internal Access Guidelines

Certain internal systems require secondary authentication credentials generated after successful completion of training.

Employees should ensure all introductory compliance modules are completed before requesting elevated permissions.
